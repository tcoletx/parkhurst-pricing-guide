Beware of very low advertised prices — they often exclude fees for the evaluation, enhancements, or ongoing care. At Parkhurst NuVision, our fee is all-inclusive.

Yes. We offer several financing plans, including 0% APR options for qualified patients. Many patients pay as little as $0 down and spread the cost over 12–24 months. We also accept HSA and FSA funds. The pricing guide covers all available options and how to apply.

Most adults 18+ with stable vision and healthy corneas are candidates for some form of vision correction. Even if traditional LASIK isn't right for you, procedures like PRK, SMILE, or EVO ICL may be. The only way to know for certain is a comprehensive evaluation — which we offer at no charge.

Standard vision insurance does not typically cover LASIK because it's considered elective. However, some employer benefit plans offer LASIK discounts, and HSA/FSA funds can be used. The pricing guide walks through all the ways to make this more affordable.
